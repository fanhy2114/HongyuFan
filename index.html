Hongyu Fan

Third year PhD Candidate of Software Engineering

I am a third year PhD Candidate at Tsinghua University School of Software, and Tsinghua-Verification Lab.

My research areas are concurrent program verification and hardware model checking. I focused on optimization of constraints solving, including SAT and SMT style, and application of partial orders to concurrent program verification. My research employs heuristics and strategies to improve the efficiency of multi-threaded program verification.

Currently, I am investigating hardware model checking with YOSYS Tool-chain, Btor2, and AIGER. I want to extend verification part of this Tool suit and apply new verification algorithms with domain-specific knowledge.
